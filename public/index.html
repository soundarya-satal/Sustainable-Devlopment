Who Uses Village360

Village Residents

Access transparent sustainability data, track community progress, and engage in data-driven advocacy for better services.

Village Leaders & Panchayats

Use VSI scores to plan targeted improvements and request government/NGO support for priority areas.

Local Government Officials

Secure portal for data updates, analysis, and evidence-based policy-making across multiple villages.

NGOs & Development Agencies

Identify underserved areas, measure intervention impact, and enhance development strategies with real data.

Role-Based Access

Citizens (View Access)

Browse sustainability data, view VSI scores, charts, and trends. Engage with transparent community information.

Verified Officials (Edit Access)

Update village data, manage sustainability metrics, and maintain accurate information for the community.

Development Partners

Access aggregated insights, identify intervention opportunities, and track development impact across regions.
