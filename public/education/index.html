About This Project

This dashboard analyzes educational quality and sustainability metrics across villages in Andhra Pradesh, India.

The data includes information about school distance, government schemes, and educational quality percentages to help identify areas for improvement.

Data Sources

The data is sourced from the andhra_pradesh_villages_education.csv file which contains information on 500 villages across multiple districts.

Metrics Explained

  • Educational Quality Percentage: A measure of the quality of education in the village
  • Village Sustainability Index: An indicator of the village's overall sustainability
  • School Distance: Distance to the nearest school in kilometers
  • Government Schemes: Active government schemes in the village
